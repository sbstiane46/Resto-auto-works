OUR STORY

Started with an admiration for e30’s. After owning several e30’s, e36’s, e46’s and really enjoying the quality, handling and power characteristics. We leaned towards exploring maintenance and upgrade modifications. Now we’ve collected vast knowledge of BMWs and deliver the quality service to freshen up street cars, modify track cars and make your BMW the ultimate driving machine as it is intended to be. We are true BMW enthusiasts.

Spec e30

Starting from a shell, we built this spec e30 from the ground up. All to specifications, this spec e30 meets the requirements to race in it's class, and it has been put to the test!

e36 Track Car

This e36 has gone through a long journey! Starting as an automatic 328i, this car has had a manual swap, chassis reinforcements, suspension overhaul, roll bar and track safety equipment installation, down-force modification, and a powertrain upgrade to an s52. This thing is a track time monster!

Turbo e30

Started as a university engineering senior exit project. This 318i, was as a neglected 318i that got rejuvenate and given steroids with a vicious turbo-charged m42! Linear power-gains reaching 300HP, suspension and brake upgrades, track safety modifications, and wheels and tires to handle the power. This car has turned many heads at the track and it can handle track time competitors!

2002 BMW

Restoration project underway. This 2002 came all original, in fairly good shape and it is a 2002 turbo themed project. This baby is getting the 2002 turbo body, upgraded brakes, a supercharged m42, and an entire rejuvenation treatment.

e30 M3

Started as a very rough e30 M3 race car chassis that was left aside. We have restored this original M3 from the ground up with all original parts and have delivered the near finished project to a client that will now take care of the powertrain; dropping an s54 in it!
